Some paper facts:

  • SS14 Paper supports upto 10 000 symbols per paper. (Including tags)
  • SS14 Paper can show upto 43 symbols per line. (Does not include tags)
  • You actually don't need to close tags for them to work.
  • Transparent pixels can be achieved either via [mono], or via [color=#0000].

How to use:

  1. Get an image (screenshot, extract, create)
  2. Crop it (21px horizontal is recommended maximum)
  3. Paste it there (Or open).
